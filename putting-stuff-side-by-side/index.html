Example 2: putting 2 dics side by side where the left div width takes 75% of the row width

row 2 left section.
flex: 3
This will take 75% of the width as 3/(3+1) is 75%
row 2 right section
flex: 1
This will take 25% of the width as 1(3+1) is 25%
